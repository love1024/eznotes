Eznotes Delivers Highly Accurate Notes In Minutes!

Benefits of lecture capture for students

Students who miss lectures because of illness or other unavoidable circumstance will have access to both the video recordings and transcribed notes

Students can listen to the recorded audio while following along with the transcribed notes

Students can revisit a lecture, refresh their memory and fill gaps they missed while in the face to face lecture

Students can access the audio recording and transcribed lecture and can rewind, replay, and follow along in real time

Students can review the lecture and go back to points the professor emphasized during class to aid with their studies

Students will be able to focus and participate more during the face to face lectures without having to worry about note taking

ESL students and students with additional learning disabilities will be able to access the recorded audio and transcribed notes at their own pace

Students can revisit the recorded lecture and review topics they did not fully grasp during the live lecture

Benefits to Universities

Provides inclusiveness and accessibility for ESL students and students with additional education needs the opportunity to personalize their individual learning

Recorded lectures can be presented once and reuse as often as needed

Recorded lectures can be used to accommodate online and distance learning

Professors will have the ability to record lectures if they will be away from campus on a given day

Increase rapport between students and professors providing opportunities for questions and problem solving in class
